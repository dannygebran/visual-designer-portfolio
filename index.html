My Process

When applying visual communication and user experience design principles, my process involves user research (persona, primary and secondary data, industrial reports analysis) in order to generate useful insights. Additionally, the use of usability tests and the continuos learning, implementating, and adapting phase for delivering genuine business value aligned to corporate objectives.

Data Define Design

In essence, knowing the user needs and wants will entirely direct the process towards best outcomes in design benefiting customers and the organisation: "Data define design"

About my work

Since I've started questioning any design solution challenging assumptions about what is the best for consumers, and observing their behaviours and developing empathy for the final user and their benefit, from that moment I knew that I was into something more complex than creating a well-presented design for my client.

Consequently, UX became of my interest, and design thinking is the fundamental framework underpinning it. Empathise, define, ideate, prototype and test conform this process which is constantly repeated for micro or macro project. Everything start with an honest user-centered sketching, prototyping, testing of ideas, learning and implementing once and again.
